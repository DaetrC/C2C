    Eternal Truth Framework

    The 6-step breakdown that powers C2C content

    1. Historical Context

    Strip away the cultural and historical moment. What was happening when this was written?

    2. Literary Context

    Understand the genre and structure. Poetry, narrative, law, prophecy—context changes meaning.

    3. Original Word Meaning

    Etymology. What did this word actually mean in Greek, Hebrew, Aramaic? Precision matters.

    4. Theological Anchor

    What does the rest of scripture say about this concept? How does this fit the larger story?

    5. Timeless/Eternal Principle

    What remains true across all eras? Strip away language, culture, time—what's eternally true?

    6. Modern Application

    How does this change the way we live, think, love, act today? Practical transformation.

    Ministry first. Clarity over hype. Structure over emotion. Christ over platform.
